Our Story

A Taste of Anatolia
in Herat

Born from a passion for Turkish culinary heritage, Neyash brings the soul of Istanbul's kitchens to the streets of Herat. Every dish — from our charcoal-grilled kebabs to our flaky baklava — is prepared with the same care found in the finest Turkish homes.

We welcome you into a casual and comfortable space, where quiet, relaxing atmosphere meets warm hospitality. Come as a guest, leave as family.
